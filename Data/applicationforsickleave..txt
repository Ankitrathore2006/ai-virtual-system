Here is a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title/Designation]
[Organization/Company]
[Organization/Company Address]

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave on [Date of Leave] as I am currently unwell and unable to come to work. I am experiencing [briefly mention your symptoms, e.g. fever, headache, etc.].

I am under medical care and am expected to recover soon. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better.

I would like to request a [number] day(s) leave starting from [Date of Leave]. I will provide a medical certificate from my doctor if required.

Thank you for considering my request. If there is any urgent work that needs my attention, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Note: You can modify this template to fit your specific needs and circumstances. It's also a good idea to keep a record of your sick leave applications and to follow your company's policies and procedures for requesting time off due to illness.
